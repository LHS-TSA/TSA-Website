Riley's Robots

Our company was founded on the belief that robotics is the future of everyday life and that humanity should not only embrace them, but we should welcome them into our lives to continue expanding our vast knowledge of electronics.

We spend countless hours developing the best personal assistants and the best AI for these assistants. Our AI is finely tuned and perfected to handle any situation someone may throw at it.

  • If our robots are commanded to inflict harm on another human being, they simply reject the command and proceed running usual, safe, tasks.

  • Our robots make their own decisions, but will never make a decision that override a human's direct command.... unless someone asks it to harm someone else.

  • Our robots use advanced neural networks to detect dangerous situations and will do everything possible to avoid harm to themselves, unless by saving themselves inflicts harm upon a human being.

Artificial Intelligence will define the future and our lives will be built around it

AI in the future:

AI will help students around the world learn new subjects they never thought they could comprehend and give struggling countries a fighting chance when it comes to educating their young.
AI also will be in technologies in the palm of our hands, not just huge robots. It will be able to translate conversations with people we haven't been able to talk to. It will drive cars, planes, trains, and anything else that moves.
AI will handle all the monotonous tasks that nobody enjoys doing and the jobs that are too dangerous for humans.

The risks of AI:

Giving robots the power to make decisions is a dangerous thing. What if it decides to turn a car off a bridge into a lake.... or to land a passenger plane on something that isn't a runway?
Before we allow any machine to make a decision that could impact the life of a human, we need to make sure that the technology is well tested so that no catastrophic errors will be made.
